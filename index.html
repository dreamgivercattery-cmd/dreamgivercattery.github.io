Who We Are

A Passion for Somali Cats

We are Nikki and Storm — a small, passionate cattery based in Johannesburg, dedicated entirely to the enchanting Somali cat breed. Once you've had a Somali in your life, it's a habit you simply can't kick.

"They are so sweet, so special, so energetic — once you've had a Somali, it's a habit you can't kick."

Our kittens are born and raised in our home, surrounded by love, people, and endless stimulation — ready to become the heart of yours.

Why Choose Us

Why Choose DreamGiver

Choosing DreamGiver means choosing a breeder who truly cares — about the cats, and about you. Here's what sets us apart.

Raised with Love

Our Top Priority

Our kittens are born on our bed and raised under our feet. They grow up surrounded by people, affection, and daily stimulation — not in cages.

Health Comes First

Vet-Checked & Vaccinated

Every kitten is vet-checked, vaccinated, and dewormed before going to their new home. They leave at 16 weeks — confident, healthy, and ready.

Well Socialised

Confident from Day One

Surrounded by people and cats of all ages, our kittens are socialised beautifully. They also enjoy a cat-oriented outdoor garden full of adventure.

Lifelong Support

We Stay in Touch

The DreamGiver relationship doesn't end at adoption. We stay in touch with every family and are always available to help, advise, and celebrate your Somali journey.
